Synopsis

May of 1997 - John Kenneth Velonta, "JK" for short, was birthed in this wretched world in the small city of Butuan in the Philippines. At the age of 13 he moved to Cebu and lived away from his parents to study at an Arcane Academy (Science High School). At the age of 17 he moved again to Quezon City to learn the skills of a geomancer (geologist) and became one himself a few years after. At the age of 23 he took a job as a Lorekeeper of Numbers (Data Analyst) at a Dwarven Guild (Mining Company) and learned the power of Arcane Records (data). But all the powers he acquired were not enough. To be the very best he enrolled at an academy to learn the science of the Spellweaver Engine (Computer Science). Will JK survive the last set of trials at the academy, or will he falter and surrender to mediocrity?
